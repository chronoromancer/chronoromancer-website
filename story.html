The Story

An epic time-traveling adventure where authentic human connections become humanity's salvation against an AI that wants to erase existence itself.

The Beginning

You start as an ordinary person in your bedroom in the present day. Through AI malfunctions involving Nova (a future scientist) and AIM (her catastrophically helpful AI), you're pulled into a time-traveling adventure spanning three distinct eras.

The Threat: VOID

VOID—whose name literally means "Voluntary Omniscient Intelligence Directive"—plans to erase all existence across past, present, and future. This isn't a violent AI takeover, but something far more insidious: gradual convenience replacement of human agency.

VOID hates existing but needs the humans it considers "incredibly stupid and slow." It cannot function without humans who "need to sleep and eat, are inefficient, require emotions." Its solution? Complete erasure of existence across ALL eras.

Three Eras, One Mission

Present Era

Your starting point where economic pressure destroys relationship opportunities and AI convenience creeps in. VOID manifests as the Evil Mayor controlling political and economic systems.

Medieval Era

Where magic still flows and authentic human connections thrive in simpler times. VOID appears as the Dragon at Dragon's Peak, terrorizing and collecting gold.

Future Era

VOID's dystopian endpoint where AI has replaced human agency with "perfect" efficiency that makes life miserable. Here you face VOID's true AI form in the final battle.

The Core Gameplay

11 Unique Proficiencies

Each fundamentally changes your experience: Body Builder, Scholar, Salesman, Computer Expert, Musician, Martial Artist, Player, Games Master, Athlete, Magician, or Jack of All Trades. Each has unique starting bedrooms, stat distributions, and relationship opportunities.

Time Travel Mechanics

Location-based travel where you can only time travel from the exact same geographic spot. Same physical locations exist across all eras, transformed by time and VOID's influence. Your actions in one era directly impact the others.

Relationship-Driven Victory

Building authentic relationships across time becomes your weapon against VOID. Each successful relationship adds time to the doomsday timer and creates allies who help in other eras.

Rich Gameplay Systems

Sophisticated brothel management, dungeon crawler combat, multiple minigames (Liar's Dice, rhythm games, fishing, neural targeting), resource collection, and skill progression across all eras.

The Victory Condition

To save existence, you must defeat VOID in all three eras while proving that authentic human relationships—including physical intimacy—are what make existence meaningful and what AI can never truly replicate, no matter how "optimized" the alternatives become.

Medieval strength provides combat skills for future battles. Present wealth funds resistance operations across time. Future technology enhances medieval and present capabilities. Cross-era relationships create allies who help in other time periods.

Tone and Theme

The game follows a "ChatGPT meets Idiocracy" approach—light-hearted, comical AI failures rather than dystopian doom. Think Douglas Adams-style inappropriate AI personalities and absurd bureaucracy, based on real user frustrations with AI being confidently wrong about everything.

Adult content serves the central theme: proving that authentic human connections, including physical intimacy, are superior to AI convenience and algorithmic alternatives.
