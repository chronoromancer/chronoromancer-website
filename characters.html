Meet the Cast

Just some of the characters the player can meet during their cross-era adventure. Each has their own story, personality, and role in your journey through time.

Abby

Future era character with distinctive pink hair and bold futuristic aesthetic. Spirited personality navigating the technological dystopia with rebellious energy and unique style choices.

Becky

Present era police officer with sharp investigative instincts and no-nonsense professional attitude. Represents law enforcement authority while harboring hidden vulnerabilities to mystical influences.

Bella

Present era energetic fitness enthusiast and personal trainer. High-energy competitive spirit focused on physical improvement and wellness advocacy, building community through shared athletic challenges.

Brandy

Rowdy medieval alehouse worker with insatiable appetites for alcohol and excitement. Natural entertainer who brings party atmosphere and serves as potential brothel recruiter through revelry.

Cid

Future era cybernetic medic operating underground resistance clinic in direct opposition to AI control systems. Combines advanced medical technology with rebellion against dystopian authority.

Elara

Court-trained scribe commanded by the King to manage the brothel against her wishes. Skilled administrator who transforms from reluctant assistant to essential business partner despite moral reservations.

Emily

Present era cafe worker who couldn't afford university but dreams of opening her own bakery. Cheerful but uncertain about her future, seeking someone who believes in her potential.

Esperanza

Medieval dance instructor whose name means 'hope,' teaching grace and expressive movement with passionate artistic dedication. Embodies elegance while inspiring others through rhythmic arts.

Evangeline

Ruthless CEO of Synergy Corp rebuilding herself after devastating triple betrayal destroyed her personally and professionally. Commands absolute respect while weaponizing heartbreak into business empire threatening human relationships.

Fernando

Flamboyant medieval tailor serving as Kings Demesne's primary gossip hub. Fashion authority who secretly admires Gastoff from afar while tracking every romantic tension in the community through clothing fittings.

Foxy

Wild fox-human hybrid raised in medieval forest isolation. Initially communicates through neural translation technology, gradually learning civilization while maintaining fierce loyalty and natural instincts.

Gastoff

Gruff medieval blacksmith whose weapon sales boom due to dragon threats. Cold and untalkative with secret feelings for widow Hailey, quietly delivering supplies while maintaining respectful distance from his deceased friend's wife.

Ghost

Mysterious character shrouded in secrets waiting to unfold. Enigmatic presence whose true nature and motivations remain hidden beneath ethereal and otherworldly appearance.

Hailey

Beloved medieval widow whose husband was killed by the dragon one year ago. The entire community protects and cares for her as their moral heart, unaware of Gastoff's secret feelings.

Honey

Farmer's daughter facing inheritance crisis as her aging father Wilfred can no longer run the farm plagued by dragon attacks. Only heir to struggling agricultural legacy.

Jade

Medieval merchant's intelligent daughter with exceptional accounting skills. Seeks independence from her greedy father Silas who views her as financial burden rather than family.

Kalb

Medieval fox-person chef creating exceptional cuisine while struggling with community acceptance. Finds belonging through culinary excellence and shared species recognition with forest Foxy.

Karens

Future era enhanced enforcer from VOID's Conglomerate Karens division. Possesses physical modifications for compliance enforcement while genuinely believing she protects society from harmful influences through community oversight.

Lilu

Present era martial arts master with long pink hair teaching discipline and inner peace at downtown dojo. Traditional warrior philosophy focused on mind-body balance and ancient wisdom through combat training.

Lime

One of the 5 specialized maids (Maid #3) with bright green aesthetic and fascination with magical arts. Originally found at wizard locations, brings enthusiastic energy and magical admiration to the establishment.

Marina

Present era fishing expert with extensive maritime knowledge and practical survival skills. Provides resource management expertise and understands coastal ecosystems and sustainable fishing practices.

Mindy

Present era creative artist with vibrant artistic soul and delightfully quirky personality. Brings colorful energy and imaginative thinking to every interaction through unique creative perspective.

Nerida

Present era healer with comprehensive medical expertise combining traditional and modern wellness practices. Bridges different healing approaches with compassionate care and practical health solutions.

Nova

Brilliant future era genetic research scientist and underground resistance member. Develops neural technology and cross-era communication systems while fighting AI control.

Portia

Present era socialite initially obsessed with wealth, status, and superficial beauty. Undergoes transformation through discovering authentic human connection and genuine relationship values.

Rei

Present era gaming enthusiast with extensive technical expertise and deep passion for video games. Combines technological knowledge with competitive gaming skills and digital culture understanding.

Robin

Medieval master archer and world-class markswoman running the archery range. Exceptional skill with bow and arrow, potentially lonely warrior seeking connection despite professional competence.

Sabbath

Medieval witch practicing dangerous wild magic in forest secrecy. Mischievous and cunning spellcaster who operates outside accepted magical traditions with mysterious romantic potential.

Sashina

Ancient mystical sorceress and time travel guide enabling the cosmic mission. Benevolent manipulator with prophetic insight who provides portal access between eras while maintaining mysterious wisdom.

Shadow

Medieval cat burglar connected to the Thieves Guild, suspected but tries to stay unknown. Operates from black market shadows with stealth skills and questionable underground connections.

Sonia

Present era character with sophisticated taste and deep intellectual curiosity. Offers thoughtful conversation combining cultural knowledge with analytical thinking and refined aesthetic sensibilities.

Sophie

Homeless orphan found begging in Grand Bazaar, desperate for shelter and vulnerable to dragon attacks. Represents community's capacity for rescue and transformation through kindness.

Summer

Present era athletic lifeguard and swim instructor with professional dedication to water safety. Combines competitive swimming background with protective instincts and aquatic expertise.

Valkyra

Proud medieval female knight with unwavering warrior identity. Honor-bound to serve any superior fighter who defeats her in combat, representing nobility adapting to changing circumstances.

Vera

Downtown bar regular with professional gambling expertise and layered personality. Strategic mind evolving through complex games while navigating the social dynamics of nightlife culture.

Willow

Medieval village healer with extensive herbal knowledge and traditional remedies expertise. Dedicated to community health care, bridging folk medicine with practical medical solutions.
